The Journey

The Salon 360
Experience

Every visit is a carefully choreographed journey from consultation to final reveal. This is not a haircut. This is a transformation.

01

Consult

Your transformation begins with a one-on-one consultation. We listen to your desires, assess your hair and skin, and craft a personalized plan that aligns with your lifestyle and vision.

02

Design

Our master stylists translate your vision into a bespoke design. Using premium products and cutting-edge techniques, we map every detail — color, cut, texture, and finish — before we begin.

03

Transform

This is where the magic happens. Skilled hands bring the design to life with precision, care, and artistry. Sit back, sip complimentary champagne, and let us work our craft.

04

Maintain

The transformation does not end when you leave our chair. We provide personalized aftercare guidance and product recommendations to keep your look flawless between visits.
