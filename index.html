Our Services

Complete Beauty Services
Under One Roof

From a fresh haircut to a total transformation, Salon 360 does it all. Our talented team stays on top of the latest trends so you always leave looking and feeling your absolute best.

Haircuts & Styling

Precision cuts for every texture and length. Whether you want a bold new look or a subtle refresh, our stylists listen first and cut second. You will love the result, guaranteed.

Color & Highlights

Vibrant, dimensional color that turns heads. From subtle highlights to bold all-over color, we use premium products that keep your hair healthy and the color lasting longer.

Balayage & Ombre

The hottest color techniques done right. Sun-kissed, natural-looking dimension that grows out beautifully. No harsh lines, no brassiness. Just gorgeous, effortless color.

Keratin Treatments

Smooth, frizz-free hair that lasts for months. Our keratin treatments repair damage while locking in shine and silkiness. Cut your styling time in half every morning.

Blowouts & Updos

Picture-perfect hair for every occasion. Bouncy blowouts for everyday glamour or elegant updos for weddings, proms, and special events. You will feel like a star.

Waxing & Threading

Smooth, precise hair removal with minimal discomfort. From brows to full body, our specialists use gentle techniques and quality waxes for clean, lasting results.

Nail Services

Flawless manicures and pedicures in a clean, relaxing environment. Choose from classic, gel, or intricate nail art. Your hands and feet deserve this level of care.

Facial Treatments

Reveal your best skin ever. Our facials target acne, aging, dryness, and dullness with professional-grade products. Walk out with a radiant, healthy glow you can see and feel.
